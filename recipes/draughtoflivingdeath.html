Draught of Living Death – The Ultimate Slumber

"A slumber profound enough to fool Death itself—yet one misstep can awaken chaos in your cauldron.

Description

This ancient and formidable potion sends its drinker into a deathlike sleep, difficult to awaken from without a skilled counter-potion. Often used in magical medical stasis or duelling trickery, it is both a marvel and a menace in the hands of the unskilled.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces wormwood infusion
  • 2 heaped teaspoons asphodel root, powdered
  • 1 sopophorous bean, crushed with silver dagger
  • 1 measure valerian root, chopped
  • ½ pinch sloth brain (for stabilizing)

Steps

  1. Warm wormwood infusion; stir twice clockwise.
  2. Add asphodel powder; stir anticlockwise until liquid darkens.
  3. Gently mix in sopophorous bean juice; temperature must not exceed a gentle simmer
  4. Fold in valerian root and sloth brain; stir seven times anticlockwise, pausing to admire the pale lilac shade.
  5. Maintain heat for 5 minutes, then extinguish flame and seal cauldron for 12 hours.
  6. Decant under moonlight for maximum potency.
