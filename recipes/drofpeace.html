Draught of Peace – Serenity in a Sip

"When one’s mind races like a Hungarian Horntail, Peace offers a haven of calm—but tread lightly, lest you conjure unexpected sparks."

Description

The Draught of Peace is a calming tonic used to ease anxiety, agitation, and sleeplessness. Its silvery vapour and gentle effect make it a favoured brew among Healers and Mind Healers alike. Though deceptively gentle in appearance, its brewing demands delicate precision.

Ingredients

  • 2 measures powdered moonstone
  • 3 drops syrup of hellebore
  • 1 teaspoon stewed mandrake root
  • A pinch porcupine quill powder
  • 1 sprig fresh valerian root, sliced

Steps

  1. Begin with moonstone in chilled water; stir counterclockwise thrice until light green hue appears.
  2. Add hellebore syrup drop by drop; watch for silvery vapour.
  3. Crumble stewed mandrake root; stir clockwise until potion warms to a soft rose.
  4. Introduce valerian slices; allow simmer for exactly 10 minutes.
  5. Reduce flame and sprinkle quill powder—potion will shift to pearlescent white.
  6. Let rest until still; bottle and label with time and date—freshness is key.
